🔥 Ember — Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

Last updated: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Toma Ovidiu-Andrei ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Ember mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the App's features:

  • Account information: Email address and password (handled securely by Supabase Auth).
  • Profile and preferences: Display name, dietary preferences, health goals, cooking time preferences, and ingredients to avoid.
  • Meal and cooking data: Recipes you save to favourites, meals you add to your planner, and your cooking history.
  • Household data: If you create or join a household, the household name and member associations.
  • Subscription status: Whether you hold an active Ember Pro subscription, managed via RevenueCat. We do not store your payment details — those are handled by Apple.

2. How We Protect Your Data

All sensitive personal data stored in our database is encrypted using AES-256-CBC encryption before being saved. Each field is encrypted with a unique random initialisation vector. The encryption key is derived per-user and is never stored in plain text. This means even if our database were accessed without authorisation, your personal information would be unreadable.

Data is stored on Supabase, a GDPR-compliant infrastructure provider, with row-level security policies ensuring each user can only access their own data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality: recipe suggestions, meal planning, cook history, and household sharing.
  • To personalise recipe recommendations based on your dietary preferences and goals.
  • To manage and verify your Ember Pro subscription status.
  • To maintain and improve the App.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (Privacy Policy) — database, authentication, and storage.
  • RevenueCat (Privacy Policy) — subscription management. RevenueCat may collect anonymised purchase analytics.
  • Apple App Store — handles all payment processing for Ember Pro. Apple's privacy policy applies to payment data.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated personal data (preferences, planner entries, cook history, favourites) is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ovidiutoma.app@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
